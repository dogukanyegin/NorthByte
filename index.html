MANIFESTO OF CORPORATE ETHICS AND PERSONA NON GRATA

As an independent and autonomous technology and defense organization, Northbyte Norway Tec Ltd. hereby establishes its non-negotiable ethical guidelines for commercial and diplomatic cooperation:

We do not engage in public executions driven by media narratives. We strictly separate actual criminals from the innocent. H.R.H. Crown Princess Mette-Marit stands as a clear example of someone unjustly judged by the public for associations entirely devoid of criminal intent. Our doors remain open to cooperation with all actors possessing clean records.

3. Geopolitical Independence and Peace Focus

Northbyte Norway Tec Ltd. operates with technological autonomy. We may engage in commercial partnerships with global technology companies, including Chinese actors, when technologically appropriate.
